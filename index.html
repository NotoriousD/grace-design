Grace Design: When Design Matters

International Design Studio
in the European Union

Welcome to Grace Design, a hub of modern interior design and innovation. Originally rooted in Ukraine in 2014. In 2022 we embarked on an exciting new chapter, evolving into an international design studio with our headquarters in Bratislava, the heart of Europe. Today, our team spans across multiple countries, with talented members based in Spain, Poland, Switzerland, and Ukraine, offering a truly global perspective to every project.

At Grace Design, we don’t just design spaces – we bring visions to life. Our expertise lies in harmonizing contemporary aesthetics with functionality, creating designs that inspire and captivate. From sophisticated residential interiors to innovative commercial spaces, our work reflects our dedication to excellence and our commitment to our clients' unique needs.

Our journey has been as diverse as our team, ranging from revitalizing historic properties to designing luxurious and bespoke residences across Europe. Every project is a story of collaboration, creativity, and a shared passion for design. Welcome to Grace Design – where your dream space becomes reality.
