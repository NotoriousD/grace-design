About Grace Design

Founded in Ukraine in 2014, Grace Design has grown into a symbol of innovation and creativity in the world of interior design. In 2022, the studio began a new chapter in the heart of Europe – Bratislava under the guidance of our new Director and Project Manager, Bohdana Kravchenko, bringing fresh vision and leadership to our work. Our team, originally based in Ukraine, has now expanded to include professionals in Spain, Poland, and even Switzerland, offering a truly global perspective to our projects.


With a collaborative team spanning multiple countries, Grace Design seamlessly blends years of experience with a forward-thinking approach, crafting spaces that inspire and tell unique stories. Driven by passion and a commitment to excellence, we continue to push boundaries and redefine the art of interior design.

Experience & Recognition

From revitalizing historic properties to crafting luxurious modern spaces across Europe, our portfolio showcases our design expertise. Recognized for excellence, our projects have been featured in leading publications and continue to set benchmarks in the world of interior design.

What We Offer At Grace Design

→ Comprehensive Interior Solutions

At Grace Design, we provide a full range of services, including interior design, project management, 3D visualization, and turnkey furnishing. From conceptual design to the final touches, we curate spaces that reflect your unique style and needs.

→ Custom-Tailored Design

Our bespoke designs for furniture, cabinetry, upholstery, and décor are crafted to bring your vision to life. Each piece is meticulously designed to harmonize with the overall concept of your space.

→ Seamless Project Management

While we don’t handle general contracting directly, we collaborate with trusted contractors across Europe. This allows us to oversee every stage of your project – from design to completion – ensuring flawless execution and timely delivery.

→ Advanced Visualization & Technology

Our expertise in cutting-edge visualization and 3D modeling tools helps you envision your future space with precision. We also use advanced project management systems to ensure transparency, efficiency, and seamless coordination at every step.

→ Turnkey Furnishing Solutions

From sourcing furniture to full-scale furnishing, we manage every detail to deliver a complete, ready-to-live space. Our network of European suppliers ensures high-quality materials and customized solutions to suit your needs.

Our Main Process:

1.

Discovery

We begin by immersing ourselves in your vision, understanding your goals, lifestyle, and preferences - both personal and business. This collaborative phase lays the foundation for a design concept that reflects your unique story, aspirations, and culture.

2.

Concept Development

Our designers craft an inspired concept that seamlessly combines aesthetics and functionality. Through meticulous attention to detail, we define the colors, textures, and key elements that bring your vision to life.

3.

Design Development

With precision and creativity, we refine layouts, optimize spaces, and create 3D models. From furniture arrangements to spatial flow, every detail is considered to ensure both beauty and practicality, while aligning with your wishes and ambitions.

4.

Material and Furniture

Leveraging our network of European suppliers, partners, and dealer agreements, we procure high-quality materials and select exquisite furniture with a keen understanding of budget considerations. Every choice is individually curated to enhance both the overall and local design, ensuring lasting value.

5.

Coordination

While we do not provide general contracting services, we collaborate with trusted contractors across Europe to ensure seamless project coordination. From overseeing installations to managing the final furnishings, we guarantee a smooth process. The result is a fully realized space, delivered turnkey and ready to inspire.
