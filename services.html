Our Interior Design Services

At Grace Design, we specialize in creating tailored interiors for both residential and commercial spaces. Our comprehensive process ensures every element is crafted to reflect your unique vision, whether it’s a cozy home, a vibrant office, or an inspiring restaurant. From initial strategy to final touches, we’re with you every step of the way.
Strategy + Concept
This phase sets the foundation for your project by defining priorities and creating a unique concept. Whether designing a home or a commercial space, we focus on the overall ambiance and experience, ensuring it aligns with your goals and style.
Design Development
We refine layouts, optimize spatial dynamics, and create harmonious arrangements of furniture, fixtures, and accessories. Leveraging 3D rendering and detailed development drawings, we ensure the space is both functional and visually captivating.
Detailed Documentation
We prepare comprehensive drawings and technical documentation that guide the construction or renovation process. These documents serve as a clear agreement between you and the contractor, ensuring precise execution aligned with the design vision.
Material and Furniture Selection
We curate a selection of premium materials, finishes, and furnishings, tailored to your style and budget. Whether it’s selecting flooring, custom cabinetry, or statement furniture, we collaborate with trusted suppliers to deliver lasting quality and exceptional design.
Construction Administration
We will help the you manage the project with the contractor during this phase. We will visit the construction site as needed to make sure the work is being done according to the plans.
Project Coordination
While we don’t provide general contracting, we work closely with experienced contractors across Europe. Our team oversees the project, ensuring smooth coordination, adherence to plans, and timely progress.
Construction Administration
We monitor the construction or renovation process, conducting site visits to ensure the project is being executed as planned. Our involvement ensures quality standards are met and every detail is handled with precision.
Installation & Styling
From placing furniture to adding final decorative touches, we manage the installation process to bring the design to life. Whether it’s a residential space or a commercial environment, we ensure the final reveal exceeds expectations and inspires everyone who enters.
